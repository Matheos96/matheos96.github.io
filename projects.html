Plump Scores [Android App]
Plump Scores is a simple application to help you swedes keep track of your "Plump" games!
It has two different modes for keeping scores and keeps a table just like you would on a traditional paper. It keeps track of whose turn it is, what the last person of the round cannot bid and at the end it calculates the total point-sum for all the players. It also asks you how many cards you want to start with per person (by default it is 10).
I wish you the best of luck in your Plump games!
The app is available for free from the Google Play Store!

Student Restaurants Turku [Android App]
Student Restaurants Turku is the only application you'll ever need to know what is for lunch in the student restaurants in the city of Turku, Finland.

Features
- See all student restaurant menus
- Choose what restaurant chains to show
- See menus for upcoming days (currently only availabe for kårkaféerna)
- Mark restaurants as favourite
- Option to only show vegetarian food
- Choose restaurant by scrolling or from drop down menu
- Choose what prices to show
- Choose wether you want to see diets or not
- Three languages available (Swedish, English and Finnish)
- Choose from 8 different themes

The app is available for free from the Google Play Store


Gas Money Calculator [Java Application]
Gas Money calculator is a simple open source java application to easily calculate how much each person will have to pay in gas money when going for a road trip.
The application automatically downloads yesterday's average fuel price (€/l) for the chosen fuel type and instantly gives you an answer based on how many people split the bill, the car's average fuel consumption and the length of the trip.
The prices are fetched from polttoaine.net, which means the prices for the downloaded fuel types will be based on Finland's average pricing from the day before. If you want to be more specific or you are not from finland, you can always use the manual input for €/l instead :)

Features
- Automatic average prices for 98E, 95E10 and Diesel (Finland)
- Manual price per litre input
- Set how many people are splitting the bill
